Yes! Our basic invoice generator is completely free with no signup required. Premium features are available for a small subscription fee.

Your invoice data stays in your browser. We don't store your information on our servers unless you choose to create an account.

Can I customize the invoice template?

Premium users can add their company logo and choose from multiple templates. We're always adding more customization options.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your account settings or by contacting us at support@makeinvoice.online.
